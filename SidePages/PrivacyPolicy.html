Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Overview

PolyKaly values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

PolyKaly is a static website and does not operate a backend. We do not actively collect personal information. The only data you provide is when you contact us directly via email.

3. Use of Information

Any information shared with us via email is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, or use your information for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new “last updated” date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@polykaly.com.
